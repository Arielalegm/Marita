Philosophy

I capture the invisible: the emotion in a glance, the sweetness of tiny hands, the strength of a united family. To me, photography is more than a beautiful image - it's a memory that speaks, that holds you, that lasts.

About me

I'm Mara, a photographer of fleeting moments that deserve to stay. I'm deeply inspired by The Little Prince, especially this quote: "All grown-ups were once children ... but only few of them remember it." I never forget it. That's why I capture childhood, love, pregnancy, and family joy with the same care we give to our most cherished memories.
